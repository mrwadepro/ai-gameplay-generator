Exploring Agricultural Education analyzes the different aspects of the
agricultural industry. This course is designed to give students a general understanding of the
importance of the agricultural industry.
Upon completion of this course, students will be able to analyze different aspects of the
agricultural industry and how it affects their daily lives. Students will have a working knowledge of
American agricultural history, Georgia agriculture, and the significance of the agricultural
education program. Students will be aware of the various career opportunities in the agriculture
industry.
